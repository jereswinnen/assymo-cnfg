Resize Anchor Point

When you resize a wall/building (e.g. increase width), which side should stay fixed?

A. Left side fixed

The left edge stays in place, building grows to the right. Like a ruler starting from the left.

B. Right side fixed

The right edge stays in place, building grows to the left.

C. Dynamic — depends on which edge you drag

Drag the right edge to grow right (left stays fixed). Drag the left edge to grow left (right stays fixed). Most intuitive for direct manipulation.

Note: Option C ties into your second request (drag-to-resize). Options A and B apply to slider-based resizing.
