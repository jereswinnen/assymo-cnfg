Selection Rectangle Style

How should the drag-to-select rectangle look on the 2D schematic?

A — Classic Blue Dashed

Semi-transparent blue fill, dashed blue border. Standard OS-style selection. Selected buildings get a blue highlight.

B — Minimal Solid

Thin solid blue border, barely-there fill. Cleaner, more modern look. Stays out of the way.

C — Marching Ants

Animated dashed border (like Photoshop). Very clear what's being selected, more "tool-like" feel.
